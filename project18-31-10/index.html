About Canon Farm

Organic is our farm motto and this is instilled onto every worker who works in the farm. It requires persistence and ongoing dedication to maintain this level in Dragonfruit farming, it is an expensive process to ensure these organic farming levels are maintained.

We house over 12 cattle, its urine and dung are harvested daily and mixed with other domestic materials like Gram flour and jiggery and left to compost, before being sieved out to create a potent liquid slurry which is fed to all the dragonfruit on a weekly basis.

We call this magical mixture “amrut” which indeed it is for our Dragonfruit farm and has made us a Gujarat Top Dragonfruit farm, and Largest organic dragonfruit farm and wholesalers in Kutch

Organic

Organic Dragon Fruit

There is no magic associated with creating an Organic fertilizer for Dragonfruit farm, it can be done using several techniques, however we have trialled a potent mixture using our sound experience in farming and our Amrut mixture is the best Organic fertilizer for Dragonfruit, Our plantation bore rich fruit within the 9 months of planting.

Such a result was highly unexpected which makes our belief in our organic fertilizer and organic dragonfruit even stronger. We have surpassed all traditional dragonfruit farming outputs achieved worldwide by almost 50% and the output results surprise us every year.
